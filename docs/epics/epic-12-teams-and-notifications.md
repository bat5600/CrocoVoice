## Story
**As a** team admin,
**I want** team workflows and notifications,
**so that** my team can adopt CrocoVoice at scale.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Team invites, join requests, and membership are supported.
2. Team dictionary entries are available to members.
3. Billing status and plan management are visible in settings.
4. Remote notifications sync to a local inbox and can be archived.
5. Local OS notifications are supported for important events.
