## Story
**As a** user,
**I want** a CrocOmni assistant with conversation history,
**so that** I can revisit and continue my assistant sessions.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. CrocOmni has a conversation view with user/assistant roles.
2. Conversations can be started, resumed, and archived locally.
3. Local storage persists conversations across restarts.
4. Messages can be added to a conversation without requiring context capture.
